Frequently Asked Questions

What is NCJ? ▼

National Cultural Jubilation (NCJ) is an annual cultural festival organized by the Notre Dame Cultural Club, celebrating art, music, dance, and drama.

When and where is the event? ▼

The 10th NCJ will be held in April 05-07 2026 at Notre Dame College.

How do I register? ▼

You can register online through our website by creating an account then by clicking the "Register" button. Choose your event category and fill in the required details.

What are the participation fees? ▼

Participation fees vary by event category. Please check individual event pages for specific fee information.

Can I participate in multiple events? ▼

Yes! Participants are welcome to register for multiple events, subject to scheduling constraints.
